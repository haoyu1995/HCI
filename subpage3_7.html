What We Do?

Overall

MentalEZ - founded in 2017 - is the warm and functional mental health website - based nonprofit dedicated to addressing the needs of those living with mental illness and to promoting the overall mental health of people.

Our services

1.Early identification and intervention for mental diseases at risk

2.Integrated care, services, and supports, like donation, being volunteer, for those who need it

3.The latest news about mental health research can immediately be discovered in our website.

4.Online chat in our website can be usable.
